Honda Shine 100 is a commuter bike available in only 1 variant and 5 colours. The Honda Shine 100 is powered by 98.98cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 7.28 bhp and a torque of 8.04 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Honda Shine 100 comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Shine 100 bike weighs 99 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 9 liters.
