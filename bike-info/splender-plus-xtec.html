Hero Splendor Plus Xtec is a commuter bike available in 6 variants and 7 colours. The Hero Splendor Plus Xtec is powered by 97.2cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 7.9 bhp and a torque of 8.05 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Hero Splendor Plus Xtec comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Splendor Plus Xtec bike weighs 112 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 9.8 liters.
