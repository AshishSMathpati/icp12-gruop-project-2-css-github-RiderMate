The Royal Enfield Himalayan 450 is an advanced iteration of the iconic adventure tourer. Built for off-road capabilities and long-distance touring, it features an all-new 450cc liquid-cooled engine that produces ample torque for challenging terrains. The Himalayan 450 boasts long-travel suspension, a rugged frame, and spoked wheels with dual-purpose tires. Equipped with a digital instrument cluster, ride-by-wire technology, and dual-channel ABS, it delivers enhanced performance and comfort for riders seeking adventure in the most demanding landscapes.
