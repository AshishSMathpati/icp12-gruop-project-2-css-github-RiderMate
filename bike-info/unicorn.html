Honda Unicorn is a street bike available in only 1 variant and 3 colours. The Honda Unicorn is powered by 162.71cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 13 bhp and a torque of 14.58 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Honda Unicorn comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Unicorn bike weighs 139 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 13 liters.
