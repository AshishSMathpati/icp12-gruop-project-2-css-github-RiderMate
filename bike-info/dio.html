Honda Dio is a scooter available in 2 variants and 5 colours. The Honda Dio is powered by 109.51cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 7.75 bhp and a torque of 9.03 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Honda Dio comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Dio scooter weighs 106 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 5.3 liters
