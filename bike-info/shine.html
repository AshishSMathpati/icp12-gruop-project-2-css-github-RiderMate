Honda Shine is a commuter bike available in 2 variants and 6 colours. The Honda Shine is powered by 123.94cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 10.59 bhp and a torque of 11 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Honda Shine comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Shine bike weighs 113 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 10.5 liters.
