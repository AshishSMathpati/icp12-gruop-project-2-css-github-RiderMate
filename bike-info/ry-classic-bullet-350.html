The Royal Enfield Bullet 350 is a motorcycle known for its rugged build and iconic styling. Retaining its traditional design cues like the hand-painted pinstripes, upright stance, and signature thumping exhaust note, the Bullet 350 is powered by a 346cc, air-cooled single-cylinder engine that generates 19.1 bhp and 28 Nm of torque. With its simplicity and old-school charm, the Bullet 350 continues to be a favorite among enthusiasts looking for a classic motorcycling experience.
