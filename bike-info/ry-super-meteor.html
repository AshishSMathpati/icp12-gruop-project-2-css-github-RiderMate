The Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 is a premium cruiser that exudes sophistication and power. Featuring a low-slung stance, wide handlebars, and a contoured seat, it is built for long-distance comfort. Powered by the same 649cc parallel-twin engine found in the 650 series, it delivers 47 bhp and 52 Nm of torque through a six-speed transmission. The Super Meteor 650 comes equipped with dual-channel ABS, LED lighting, and alloy wheels, offering a luxurious and effortless riding experience with a touch of classic elegance.
