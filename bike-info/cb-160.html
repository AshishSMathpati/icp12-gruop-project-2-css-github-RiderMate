Honda CB350 is a cruiser bike available in 2 variants and 10 colours. The Honda CB350 is powered by 348.66cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 20.7 bhp and a torque of 29.4 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Honda CB350 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This CB350 bike weighs 186 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 15.2 liters.
