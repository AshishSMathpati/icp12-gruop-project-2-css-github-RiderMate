Honda Hornet 2.0 is a street bike available in only 1 variant and 4 colours. The Honda Hornet 2.0 is powered by 184.4cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 17.03 bhp and a torque of 15.9 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Honda Hornet 2.0 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Hornet 2.0 bike weighs 142 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 12 liters.
