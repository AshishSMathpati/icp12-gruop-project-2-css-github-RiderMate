Hero Xoom 160 is a scooter available in only 1 variant and 3 colours. The Hero Xoom 160 is powered by 156cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 14.6 bhp and a torque of 14 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Hero Xoom 160 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Xoom 160 scooter weighs 142 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 7 liters.
