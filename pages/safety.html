Ways to Ride a Bike Safely

  • Always wear a helmet.
  • Use proper hand signals.
  • Check your brakes before riding.
  • Wear bright or reflective clothing.
  • Follow all traffic rules and signals.
  • Stay alert and avoid distractions.
  • Use bike lanes when available.
  • Keep both hands on the handlebars.
  • Ride in the same direction as traffic.
  • Inspect your bike regularly for damage.

What If You Don’t Ride Safely?

  • Higher risk of serious injury or death.
  • Increased chances of collisions with vehicles.
  • Damage to your bicycle or others’ property.
  • Legal consequences if rules are broken.
  • Loss of confidence or fear of riding again.
