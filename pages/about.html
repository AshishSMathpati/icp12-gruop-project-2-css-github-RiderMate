About Us

Budget-friendly & seat-sharing

As a passenger, you pay only for the seat not the entire ride making daily commutes much more affordable. As a driver, you can save on fuel or even earn by sharing empty seats. This helps offset your costs for regular travel

Reduces traffic & pollution

By encouraging carpooling, Rider Mate helps reduce the number of vehicles on the road, easing traffic congestion and lowering carbon emissions




Our Mission

To deliver an affordable, secure, and eco-friendly carpooling solution that connects drivers and passengers seamlessly—helping reduce fuel costs, ease traffic, and lower carbon emissions while fostering safe, trusted daily commutes.
